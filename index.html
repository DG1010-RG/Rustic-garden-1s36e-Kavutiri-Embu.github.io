Core Agenda

Rooted in the fundamental rights of health and food security, we focus on structure and research.

🌿 Functional Health

Integration of researched herbal medicine into standard care. We focus on the benefits of essential oils and tinctures through mass education and controlled manufacturing.


🌾 Agricultural Economy 2050

Reducing national reliance on imports by conducting deep research into native crops and soil health. Agriculture is the foundation of a nation's independence.


🛡️ Modernized Security

A move from intimidation to surveillance. We advocate for patrol structures using drones and modern technology to ensure community peace without fear.


🎨 Cognitive Investment

Establishing public Music and Art Centers. Investing in the cognitive capacities of children today ensures a better-built tomorrow.
