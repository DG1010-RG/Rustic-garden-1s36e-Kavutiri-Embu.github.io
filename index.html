Environmental Interface

Operating from Kavutiri, Embu-Kenya, Rustic Garden is a vibrant, open space dedicated to observing land patterns, tracking moisture, and implementing restorative social systems. We make modern research tools approachable and human-centered.

🚜Field Events

Movement and action are at our core. We host agricultural field days, participatory research, and interactive learning for all ages and abilities.

🔬Research Programs

Focusing on cognitive growth, circular agriculture, and developing locally relevant herbal extracts—accessible science that uplifts everyone.

Core Agenda

Your right to health and food security matters. We build agricultural and social structures on evidence, not just words.

🌿Functional Health

We educate on researched herbal medicines and bring essential oils/tinctures to communities—safely, accessibly, and openly.

🌾Agricultural Economy 2050

We investigate native crops, soil health, and create data that leads to food security—research before rhetoric, observation before action.

🛡️Modernized Security

Fostering peace and safety using technology—like drones—not intimidation. Surveillance that builds trust and community.

🎶Cognitive Investment

Music and art centers for youth—your mind's growth is the community's future.
