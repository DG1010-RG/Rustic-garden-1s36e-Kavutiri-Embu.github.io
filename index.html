02 — Core Agenda

What we stand for

Rooted in the fundamental rights of health and food security, every pillar of Rustic Garden is built on evidence, not rhetoric.

01

🌿 Functional Health

Integration of researched herbal medicine into standard care. We focus on the benefits of essential oils and tinctures through mass education and controlled manufacturing — bridging traditional knowledge with modern verification.

02

🌾 Agricultural Economy 2050

Reducing national reliance on imports by conducting deep research into native crops and soil health. Agriculture is the foundation of a nation's independence — and Embu's highland soils are among Kenya's most fertile.

03

🛡️ Modernized Security

A move from intimidation to surveillance. We advocate for patrol structures using drones and modern technology to ensure community peace without fear — protecting both people and crops.

04

🎨 Cognitive Investment

Establishing public Music and Art Centres. Investing in the cognitive capacities of children today ensures a better-built tomorrow. Creativity and agriculture are not opposites — they are partners.

04 — Information

Common questions

Rooted in observation and action. We believe structure is the antidote to the current worrisome state of healthcare and food security. Before prescribing, we measure. Before planting, we study the soil. Before advocating, we verify.
We prioritise research over rhetoric. We track the moisture in the soil before we plant the seed of change. Every program we run produces data, and every dataset informs the next program. It is a closed loop of learning.
The Rosemary represents healing, memory, and medicinal heritage — a plant that thrives in difficult soil. The Bee represents industry, community, and the pollination of ideas across people and places. Together, they are the spirit of Rustic Garden.
Our primary base is in Kavutiri, Embu County, Kenya — in the highland region east of Mount Kenya. The landscape, soil type, and community culture of this area are central to all our research.
We welcome institutional, corporate, and private partnerships. Whether you contribute land, funding, expertise, or platforms — reach out via the contact section below and our team will respond within a week to explore alignment.
